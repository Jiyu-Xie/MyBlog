* *The Zhiren Baking Room* is a student group created to promote the appreciation and practice of baking at SUSTech in 2013, where the name of Zhiren is chosen from the Zhiren College, which embodies the spirit of "inviting knowledge, making the heart and inspiring the heart." 

* It is an outlet for students and other members of the SUSTech community to express their creativity with others who share this interest. We set out to bring cooks together from all over to exchange recipes and ideas and to support each other in the kitchen in SUSTech. 

* Whether you have a skillful baking talent or want to try your hand at baking for the first time, the Zhiren Baking Room is open to people in SUSTech of all levels and fields of baking. We wanted to create a buzzing place for others who do want do what we do all day long: talk about food! 

### Constituent Organization ###

Now, we have two bakery managers, Cheng Tao(陶程) and Minmin Lin(林敏敏), and there are three depantments in the Baking Room -- activities, publicity and supervision, respectively. The Zhiren Baking Room offers a DIY platform for its bakers and baking assistants.
	
### The Tutorial System ###

In the Zhiren Baking Room, teaching is conducted primarily through the tutorial system. Normally, we have five tutorials a week, and each tutorial consists of a two-hour meeting between the tutor(baker) and small numbers of students(baking assistants) (usually one to six). During this time, a baking project prepared specifically for that tutorial is done mainly by students and commented upon by the tutor. Tutorials have gained their reputation because of the close relationship they maintain between the tutor and the student, and these baking skills have also been passed down from generation to generation.
	
### Events & Activity ###

Every year, the Zhiren Baking Room hosts an extraordinary variety of events—we have Baking Festival every new semester, we make mooncakes for the Mid-Autumn Festival, we sell what we bake to the whole community, we bake the birthday cakes for the public, and we even bake the birthday cake for our headmaster Shiyi Chen as a special gift.
